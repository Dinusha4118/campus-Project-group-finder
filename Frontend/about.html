About UniConnect

Connecting students for better collaboration and academic success.

Our Mission

Uniconnect was created to solve the common problem students face when trying to form project groups. We believe that collaboration is key to academic success, but finding the right teammates can be challenging.

Our platform makes it easy to connect with fellow students who share your academic interests, skills, and goals, ensuring that every project is a success.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

To create a group, log in to your account and click on the "Create Group" button on the Groups page. Fill in the required information about your project, including the topic, description, and required skills. Once submitted, your group will be visible to other students who can request to join.

Is Uniconnect free to use?

Yes, Uniconnect is completely free for all students. Our platform is supported by the university to enhance student collaboration and learning experiences.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our matching algorithm considers several factors including your department, skills, academic year, and project preferences. It then suggests groups that align with your profile and displays them in your recommendations.

Can lecturers monitor groups?

Yes, lecturers have access to an admin panel where they can view group compositions, progress, and provide feedback. However, they cannot directly participate in group communications unless invited by the group members.

What if I have issues with my group?

If you're experiencing issues with your group members, you can use our conflict resolution feature to report the problem. Our team will review the situation and provide mediation if necessary. You can also leave a group at any time if it's not working out.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your profile settings and click on "Account Settings." From there, you'll find the option to delete your account. Please note that this action is irreversible and will remove all your data from our system.
